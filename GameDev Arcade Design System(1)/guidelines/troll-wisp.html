LIDÉRC · Wisp

Apró, gyors, félig áttetsző — cikázva közelít
Build: small body sphere at low opacity (transparent, opacity .55), rune-deep #16414f material, lit from within by an emissive #36c5ff core + a bright glow sprite (same trick as the glow-mushrooms). Wispy trailing tail (a few shrinking spheres). Eyes/core cyan, not orange.
Motion: fast zig-zag darts; bobs vertically. Reads best on: ice (freezes the wisp solid — opacity → 1) & poison (the inner glow sickens cyan→green). Low HP, low score, comes in packs.
