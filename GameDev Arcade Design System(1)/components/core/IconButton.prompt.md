Round game control for emotes and actions (the circular buttons floating over the play area in Mohás Berci) — holds an emoji or icon, with an optional tiny caption.

```jsx
<IconButton tone="smash" size="lg" label="Zúz">💥</IconButton>
<IconButton tone="glass">👋</IconButton>
```

Tones: `glass` (frosted, for emote rows), `smash` (orange radial — primary action), `roar` (blue radial — secondary). Sizes `sm`(42) `md`(62) `lg`(92). Press shrinks to .88 and brightens. Use the brand's emoji vocabulary (👋 🕺 😂 💥 🗯️) — emoji ARE on-brand for the cozy games.
